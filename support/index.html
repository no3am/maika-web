Common Questions

How do I cancel my Maika+ subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).

  • iPhone/iPad: Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Maika → Cancel Subscription
  • Android: Open Google Play → tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Maika → Cancel

How do I delete my account and data?

Open the Maika app → Profile tab → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account". This permanently deletes your account and all health data immediately.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions sent to your email.

My cycle prediction seems wrong. What should I do?

Cycle predictions improve over time as you log more data. Make sure your period start and end dates are logged accurately each month. After 3+ cycles, predictions become significantly more accurate. If something seems consistently wrong, email us and we'll help.

The AI chat isn't working. What's wrong?

The AI chat feature requires an active Maika+ subscription. If you have Maika+, check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, email us with your account email address.
